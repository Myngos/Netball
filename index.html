About Namibia Volleyball

The Namibia Volleyball Federation (NVF) is the governing body for volleyball in Namibia. Established in 1996, we've been dedicated to growing the sport across our beautiful nation.

Our mission is to develop volleyball at all levels, from schools and local clubs to our national teams. We organize competitions, provide training for players and coaches, and work to increase the visibility of volleyball in Namibia.

Join us in our journey to make Namibia a powerhouse in African volleyball!
